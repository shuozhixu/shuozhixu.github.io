Group

The Computational Materials, Mechanics, and Manufacturing Laboratory (CM3 Lab) at the University of Oklahoma aims at guiding the design and manufacturing of high-performance structural and functional materials via physics-based and artificial-intelligence-based approaches from subatomic to macro scale.
