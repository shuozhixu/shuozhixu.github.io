Group

The Computational Materials, Mechanics, and Manufacturing Laboratory (CM3 Lab) at the University of Oklahoma aims at guiding the design and manufacturing of high-performance structural and functional materials via multi-physics simulations from subatomic to macro scale and data-driven methods such as machine learning.
