Research

I am the PI of the computational materials, mechanics, and manufacturing lab (CM3 Lab) at the University of Oklahoma. The vision of our lab is to guide the design and manufacturing of high-performance structural materials via multi-physics simulations from subatomic to macro scale and data-driven methods such as machine learning.
