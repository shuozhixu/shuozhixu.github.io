Mentoring

If you are an undergraduate student looking for an internship in the Beyerlein group, please email Dr. Irene J. Beyerlein. UC Santa Barbara provides a variety of opportunities for undergraduate research. Most common programs are

  • CAMP - summer and school year, UC Santa Barbara students
  • FLAM - summer, both UC Santa Barbara and non UC Santa Barbara students
  • RISE - school year, UC Santa Barbara students

If you are an intern in the Beyerlein group, these webpages may be useful:

  • Using LAMMPS at UC Santa Barbara
  • Writing an academic paper
